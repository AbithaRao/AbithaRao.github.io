Nawal Mustafa

Nawal Mustafa is a doctorial student and a researcher in clinical neuropsychology. Fascinated by how the biology and psychology of the human brain influences one's behaviour, Nawal runs @thebraincoach on Instagram to stand up to the stigma around mental health.

On her handle, she provides easy access to mental health tips to all those who don't have the courage or resources to seek professional help, impacting almost 313K lives. She creates insightful therapeutic content that resonates with what all of us go through frequently.

Join us in an exclusive conversation with Nawal Mustafa on this month's TED Circle with the theme "Leading with Curiosity" on October 2nd, 6:30 pm IST.

Edition 3.0 (2019)

Parth Thakur

Partth Thakur is the founder of the non-profit organization, 'The Wishing Factory' which aims at fulfilling wishes and supporting healthcare of the underprivileged Thalassemia Major Patients in India.

Diagnosed with Thalassemia Major right after birth, Partth takes blood every 15 days from 3 donors. Partth firmly believes that this 'borrowed life' can be made meaningful by giving back and putting smiles on faces. @thewishingfactory has so far helped fulfill 450+ wishes of Thalassemia Major Warriors & supported Healthcare of 3000+ Warriors.

Join us in conversation with @i_partththakur about his journey on this month's TED Circle with the theme: "Designing with Humanity".

Anubha Maneshwar

Anubha Maneshwar is a young entrepreneur- the founding director of GirlScript Foundation, which is India's biggest tech education organisation. Operating in 56 cities across India, her company has impacted nearly 30,000 students in just 2.5 years of its inception. The organization has received Elastic Search Cause Awards 2019 at Sydney Australia.

She is a speaker at TEDx, PyCon North America, WWTech Summit Washington DC and more than 150 other national and international events.

She is a recipient of the Indian Women Leadership Excellence Award for Education, 35 Under 35 Best Startups Award in Education, India's Top 100 Young Leaders Award and Top 8 across the globe for Technology Playmaker Award by Booking.com at London. Recently, she has qualified to the Forbes Asia 30 Under 30 list for the social entrepreneurship category. We're honoured to have her as a part of our TED Circle conference!
